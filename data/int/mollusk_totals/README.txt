## Intermediate Mollusk Data ##

Compare intermediate data with USDA 2013 Census of Aquaculture summary report (page 10): http://www.aquafeed.com/documents/1412204142_1.pdf



## Notes ##
US - Species: MOLLUSKS - Product_Type: ALL PRODUCTS
These data are total sales and production for all mollusk products in the US

All Product Types: ALL PRODUCTS
All Mollusks: MOLLUSKS

Product_Type sub-catgories: Broodstock, Foodsize, Larvae and Seed, Other Sizes

Species sub-categories: Abalone, Clams, Oysters, Mussels

Oyster sub-categories: Other Oysters, Eastern Oysters, Pacific Oysters
Clams sub-categories: Other Clams, Manila Clams

The Species values are categories of data. Each row is not necessary a stand alone observations. Some rows are subsets of other rows. For example, Species = Clam and Product_Type = All Products provides values for all clams and all products (seed, broodstock, foodsize)



## File Descriptions ##
## US_sales_all_tidy
Tidied, easily filterable data table. Includes all three census years.

## US_sales_2013
Only includes 2013 census year and individual states (filtered out total US data)

## US_sales_gapfill
Only includes 2013 census year and individual states as well as gapfilled values.

## US_sales_NA_2013
Num of NA values per data type

## US_sales_per_operation
Average sales per op per census year
